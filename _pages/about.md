## Maciel M. Queiroz

Associate Professor  
FGV EAESP

Maciel is an Associate Professor at Fundação Getulio Vargas – EAESP (FGV EAESP). He also serves as Ambassador for Latin America (South) of the Operations and Supply Chain Management Division of the Academy of Management (OSCM–AOM) and Associate Editor of the International Journal of Management Reviews (ABS 3, ABDC A).

He is Senior Associate Editor of the International Journal of Logistics Management (ABS 1, ABDC A) and Regional Editor of the Journal of Knowledge Management (ABS 2, ABDC A). In addition, he is a *member of the Editorial Board of the International Journal of Operations and Production Management (ABS 4, ABDC A), the Journal of Business Logistics (ABS 3, ABDC A), and the International Journal of Information Management (ABS 2, ABDC A)**.

Maciel holds a Ph.D. (2017) and M.Sc. (2011) in Naval Architecture and Ocean Engineering from the Polytechnic School of the University of São Paulo, a Specialization in Logistics from Mackenzie Presbyterian University (2008), and a B.A. in Business Administration from Centro Universitário de Santo André (2006).

He has been recognized in the Stanford/Scopus–Elsevier “World’s Top 2% Most Cited Scientists” ranking in 2022, 2023, 2024, and 2025.
