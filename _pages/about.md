### Maciel M. Queiroz

**Associate Professor**  
FGV EAESP – São Paulo School of Business Administration 
São Paulo, Brazil

<hr style="border: none; height: 3px; background-color: #cccccc;">

Maciel M. Queiroz is an Associate Professor at Fundação Getulio Vargas – EAESP (FGV EAESP). He also serves as Ambassador for Latin America (South) of the Operations and Supply Chain Management Division of the Academy of Management (OSCM-AOM) and Associate Editor of the *International Journal of Management Reviews*.

He is Senior Associate Editor of the *International Journal of Logistics Management* and Regional Editor of the *Journal of Knowledge Management*. In addition, he serves on the Editorial Board of the *International Journal of Operations and Production Management*, the *Journal of Business Logistics*, and the *International Journal of Information Management*.

He has been recognized in the Stanford/Scopus-Elsevier **World’s Top 2% Most Cited Scientists** ranking in 2022, 2023, 2024, and 2025.
